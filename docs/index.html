COMMON QUESTIONS, CLEAR ANSWERS

What types of businesses does SiuCloud primarily serve? ▼
We are laser-focused on **Small to Medium-sized Businesses (SMBs)** across a diverse range of industries, including e-commerce, healthcare, financial services, tech startups, and professional services. We understand the unique challenges SMBs face, such as budget constraints and limited in-house IT resources, and tailor our solutions specifically to these needs.
Which cloud platforms are within your area of expertise? ▼
Our deep expertise covers the leading public cloud providers: **Amazon Web Services (AWS), Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**. Additionally, we have extensive experience with on-premise virtualization solutions like VMware vSphere, allowing us to effectively manage and integrate hybrid cloud environments.
How can SiuCloud significantly reduce my cloud expenses? ▼
We employ a multi-faceted approach to cost optimization. This includes performing in-depth cloud cost audits to pinpoint wasteful spending, recommending and implementing efficient pricing models (like Reserved Instances or Savings Plans), right-sizing your resources, and advising on serverless architectures where they can provide maximum cost-efficiency. Our goal is always to maximize your cloud ROI.
Do you offer continuous cloud management or support? ▼
While our primary focus is strategic advisory and detailed analysis, we empower your team for ongoing management. We can recommend top-tier managed service providers, assist in training your internal IT staff, and crucially, help you establish robust FinOps (Cloud Financial Operations) practices for continuous cost monitoring and optimization within your organization.
What truly sets SiuCloud apart from other cloud consulting firms? ▼
Our distinct advantage lies in our unwavering focus on SMBs. We provide practical, actionable, and results-driven advice without the overwhelming jargon or unnecessary complexity often found with larger firms. Our founder, Roberto Siu, personally brings **over a decade of hands-on, practical cloud experience** to every client engagement, ensuring tailored solutions and tangible business value. We are your dedicated cloud partners.
